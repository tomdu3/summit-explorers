Our Next Adventure

Cnicht & Moelwyn Mawr

Saturday 21st June

Join us for one of Snowdonia's hidden gems – a thrilling double-summit route that includes the iconic Cnicht (often called the "Welsh Matterhorn") and the peaceful, panoramic ridge over Moelwyn Mawr. It's a walk packed with drama, beauty, and views that stretch to the sea.

Grade: Moderate – with a few steep bits but lots of stops and support
Distance: ~11km (7 miles)
Duration: Approximately 6-7 hours
